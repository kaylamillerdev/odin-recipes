Chicken Pot Pie

Tender chicken and vegetables in a creamy sauce surrounded by buttr=ery, flaky crust

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Mixed Vegetables
  • Cream of Chicken Soup
  • Pie Crust
  • Water (enough to boil the chicken thighs in)
  • Chicken Boulion Cubes
  • Melted Butter (for the crust)
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Place chicken thighs, water and chicken boulion cubes in a pot.
  2. Turn pot on medium high heat and keep an eye on it. Bring the chicken thighs to a bouil and cook until just about done.
  3. Once the chicken is done boiling, place it on a plate or in a bowl and park it in the fridge or freexer for a few minutes, just until it's cool enough to handle.
  4. Pull the skin and chicken off the bones. Set the skin aside and put the chicken in a large pot or wide pan.
  5. Place the mixed vegetables, cream of chicken soup and some of the water from the chicken thighs in the pot / pan.
  6. bring all this to a warm temp.
  7. dump into a pie plate lined with pie crust and spread evenly. Place the second crust on top, and crimp with either your fingers or a fork. Cut slits or poke the top with a fork to allow steam to escape.
  8. cover with aluminum foil and place into the oven for 30 mins.
  9. Cook time may vary depending on oven. Start checking your food frequently after this 30 mins. Also, remove the foil from the top to allow the crust to brown.
